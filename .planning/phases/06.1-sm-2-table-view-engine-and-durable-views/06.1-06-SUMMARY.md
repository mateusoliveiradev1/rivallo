## Decisions Made

- Keep the selector fully controlled and structurally typed at the UI boundary; it receives validated view data and callbacks but imports no client, repository, storage, migration, Tauri, or mutation implementation.
- Keep persistent failures visible through Status and confirmed migrations brief through Toast, with one canonical live region per outcome.
- Translate command rejection codes through closed product copy and schema labels; never render internal paths, codes, details, payloads, or app-data locations.
- Preserve dirty text until confirmed save/reset and require explicit save, discard, or continue-current decisions before destructive navigation or active deletion.
