## Decisions Made

- A customizer close restores the exact proposal present when the surface opened; `Restaurar colunas` changes columns only, while `Restaurar visualização` applies the complete saved baseline.
- Normal clicks replace/cycle the primary sort; Shift preserves and cycles ordered multi-sort clauses up to the schema-owned three-clause bound.
- Pinned side offsets are computed only from normalized visible finite widths and explicit pin order; no translated label, array index, or CSS child position owns identity or geometry.
- Repository unavailability and persistence failure remain the existing controller/header product states; the customizer owns no duplicate storage or diagnostic authority.
