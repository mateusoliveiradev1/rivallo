# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary users are adult football-management players running a club from a desktop during focused, data-dense sessions. The initial online audience is invited friends in small private leagues.

## Product Purpose

Rivallo makes strategic club management feel precise, responsive, and trustworthy in both local careers and shared online leagues. Success begins with a reliable private multiplayer proof, not feature breadth.

## Positioning

A premium football management command centre whose online competition remains trustworthy because the server, not the client, decides consequential outcomes.

## Brand Personality

Strategic, confident, immersive. It should feel like a command room under match-night floodlights: composed under pressure, never like betting, esports neon, or a generic SaaS dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid visual copies of Football Manager, EA FC, FootSim, and Brasfoot; default shadcn styling; nested cards; decorative glass; casino cues; and mobile patterns enlarged for desktop.

## Design Principles

1. Authority is visible: offline, sync, and connection states are explicit.
2. Dense information earns its space through hierarchy and table craft.
3. Interaction is fast, familiar, keyboard-capable, and purposeful.
4. Club colour is contextual data, never the product identity.
5. The working name and identity remain reversible until approved.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support full keyboard use, visible focus, reduced motion, semantic tables and dialogs, contrast validation, zoom, long text, and future localisation.
