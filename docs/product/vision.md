# Vision

Rivallo is a premium football-management command centre for desktop. Its differentiator is trustworthy shared competition: a player's device may be offline temporarily, but it never becomes the final authority for online sporting decisions.

## Release scope

**Milestone 0:** documentation, workspace foundation, quality gates, minimal desktop/API, local and Docker PostgreSQL preparation, design system, and basic observability. **V0.1:** dev identity; fictional club choice; dashboard/squad; SQLite restore/cache; minimal API; explicit offline behavior. **V0.2:** real minimal auth, private league/invite, two users, unique club selection, authoritative fictitious round, reconnection and Neon persistence. **V1:** small private alpha leagues with calendar, simple squad/line-up, audit and reliable sync. Complex simulation and management systems are later.
